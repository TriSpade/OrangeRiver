Monitoring the water quality of watersheds, rivers and streams

Intelligent River Operates several hydrological observation systems on the Savanah River through out South Carolina and Georgia.

Analyzing the quality of watersheds, rivers and streams

Intelligent River and Orange River takes data obtained through monitoring, and looks for outliers, patterns, and seeks to apply machine learning techniques to better predict anomaloies and trends.

Providing information in the field of water quality

The overall goal of both groups is to provide useful data that can further research, development and the conservation of water through out the world.
