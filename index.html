Intelligent River: From Observational to Operational

Learn More

Monitoring the water quality of watersheds, rivers and streams

Intelligent River operates several hydrological observation systems on the Savanah River through out South Carolina and Georgia.

Analyzing the quality of watersheds, rivers and streams

Intelligent River and Orange River take data obtained through monitoring, and look for outliers, patterns, and seek to apply machine learning techniques to better predict anomalies and trends.

Providing information in the field of water quality

The ultimate goal of the collaboration is to provide useful data that can further research, development, and conservation of water throughout the world.
